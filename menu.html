Our Menu

Whether you choose to indulge in a pizza, savor the luxury of lobster, or explore the delicate artistry of sushi, our menu at Our Global Restaurant promises to take your taste buds on a delightful culinary journey filled with exquisite flavors and culinary craftsmanship.

Pizza

From classic Margherita with its simplicity and freshness to adventurous combinations like truffle-infused mushroom and prosciutto, our pizzas are crafted to satisfy all taste buds. Each pizza is prepared with a thin, crispy crust and topped with premium cheeses, savory sauces, and a variety of delectable toppings, creating a perfect harmony of flavors.

Lobster

We offer a range of exquisite lobster dishes, from butter-poached lobster tails to lobster bisque. The succulent and tender meat of the lobster is expertly prepared and paired with complementary ingredients, such as herb-infused butter or tangy citrus glaze. Whether you prefer a classic lobster roll or an elegant lobster thermidor, our menu celebrates the delicate and decadent flavors of this prized seafood.

Sushi

From traditional favorites like salmon and tuna to more adventurous options like sea urchin or eel, our sushi menu caters to both purists and those seeking unique flavor combinations. Each piece is expertly prepared by skilled sushi chefs, ensuring a symphony of taste and texture with every bite.
